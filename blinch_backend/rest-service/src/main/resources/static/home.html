Inclusion, inspiration  & happiness at work

Blinch is an app that connects two collegues to share a lunch break together. It helps employees to share knowledge, be inspired and collaborate interdisciplinary.

“Happiness is a state of activity.”

– Aristotele

Inclusion

Imagine colleagues you don’t work with on a daily basis want to get to know you, but don’t know if it’s appropriate to ask you to go for a lunch. Or you want to ask and feel akward.


Inspiration

Imagine during lunch you spread the word about a project you’re working on. Your colleague got a helpful link or connection that not only made your work easier, it inspired you to a whole new level.


Happiness

Imagine no matter in which corner of the office you look, the people make you smile because you share fun lunch memories.
